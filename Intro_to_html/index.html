Why Ramen?

Are you hungry, maybe just need a snack? Don't have a lot of time and don't want to spend much money? Luckily for you, there's an answer. Keep reading below to learn how to make ramen noodles and the best brands to try.

Items and ingredients needed

  • Medium sauce pan
  • Electric or gas stove
  • Ramen noodles with flavoring packet
  • 2 cups water
  • Optional: vegetables, eggs, sauces, etc.

General Cooking Instructions

To prepare a bowl of ramen, start by bringing 2 cups of water to a boil in a medium saucepan. Gently add the ramen noodles to the water, make sure they are completely submurged. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes, or until they are tender yet slightly firm to the bite. Open the included seasoning packaet and add it's contents to the pot. Add any other optional ingredients for more depth of flavor. Be sure to let your noodles cool before eating.
